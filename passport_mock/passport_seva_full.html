Photograph Guidelines

Passport photographs must meet these specifications to avoid rejection

✅ Accepted Photo Requirements
  • Size: 51mm × 51mm (2 inches × 2 inches)
  • Background: Plain white or off-white background
  • Face: Full face, front view, eyes open
  • Head Position: Centered in frame, 1–1⅓ inch face height
  • Expression: Neutral, mouth closed
  • Glasses: Not allowed (since 2016)
  • Head Covering: Not allowed (except for religious reasons)
  • Print: Colour photo on matte finish paper
  • Age: Photo must be taken within last 6 months
  • Infants: Eyes open, no supporting hand visible
❌ Common Rejection Reasons
  • Blurry, grainy or pixelated photo
  • Shadows on face or background
  • Sunglasses or tinted glasses
  • Hair covering the face or eyes
  • Hat, cap or head covering (non-religious)
  • Edited / digitally altered photo
  • Black & white or sepia toned photo
  • Torn, creased or stapled photo
